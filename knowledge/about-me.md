## Who I Am

I'm a business leader and entrepreneur with 30 years of experience working at the intersection of technology, data, and creativity. I've spent my career helping companies grow — from co-founding and scaling one of the world's first programmatic media platforms, to leading large APAC practices at Accenture and WPP, to now running my own advisory firm and a portfolio of early-stage investments.

I founded **Verdena** in 2025 to do what I do best: help organisations across Asia Pacific accelerate growth by aligning marketing, sales, data, and technology with commercial strategy and real-world execution. Alongside that, I'm building **ZoomOut Project** — an ecosystem that inspires and engages kids around the future of work, based on their passions, starting with a series of picture-story books I'm writing.

Beyond client work, I'm an active contributor to the industry as an author, keynote speaker, and panelist — particularly on the intersection of AI, data, creativity, and their impact on business growth.

## How I Work

### What drives me

I'm results-driven and execution-focused. Ideas alone don't move the needle — I've seen that first-hand throughout my career. Fast, high-quality execution is what separates growth from stagnation. I'm also a fast learner: I've thrived working across industries, geographies, and business models, always finding ways to absorb new contexts quickly and apply them commercially.

### My strengths (CliftonStrengths Top 5)

I lead with **Influencing** themes:

1. **Woo** — I love meeting new people and winning them over. I'm energised by building networks and connecting people.
2. **Positivity** — I bring contagious enthusiasm and an upbeat outlook that tends to lift teams and rooms.
3. **Futuristic** — I'm a visionary who's naturally oriented toward what could be, and I energise others with that vision.
4. **Activator** — I turn thoughts into action. I want to make things happen now, not talk about them indefinitely.
5. **Communication** — I find it easy to put complex ideas into clear, compelling words — in conversation, on stage, or in writing.

### Personality

**Enneagram Type 4 — The Individualist.** I bring emotional depth and a strong drive for authenticity to everything I do. I'm introspective and self-aware, with a deep need for meaning and genuine connection in both work and relationships. I resist conformity and seek to express an original perspective. I do my best work in environments where I can bring creativity, autonomy, and real substance to the table.

### How others describe working with me

Feedback from colleagues, clients, and teams consistently highlights a few things:

- **Exceptional at building trust quickly** — I put clients at ease without coming across as selling. I'm credible from the first meeting, and people open up about real problems.
- **Strategic and tactical in equal measure** — I can articulate a vision for a room and then roll up my sleeves to get it done. Multiple collaborators have noted I'm unusually willing to go deep on details even at a senior level.
- **A genuine leader and mentor** — Teams describe me as humble, listening with intent, making space for everyone's voice, and having their backs even when it's inconvenient. I'm never fully satisfied — there's always a "what's next" — but that restlessness keeps teams sharp and thinking big.
- **A network builder** — I've built and led some of the most engaged CMO communities in Asia, and consistently bring the right people into the room.
- **Courageous and resilient on complex deals** — Rated 5/5 across multiple Client Lead capability assessments, particularly as a Trusted Strategic Partner, Inspirational Team Leader, and Industry Expert.
